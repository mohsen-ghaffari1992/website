I am a postdoctoral researcher in Computer Science at SQUARE group, IT-University of Copenhagen, Denmark. I received a PhD degree in Computer Science from ITU. My research focuses on using formal methods to improve the reliability, safety, and explainability of Reinforcement Learning. I also explore decision-making under uncertainty and the applications of Multi-Agent Reinforcement Learning in real-world problems, particularly in smart grids and route planning.

I am open to work and seeking for a Postdoctoral role focusing on Reinforcement Learning, Multi-agent Systems, and Decision Making under uncertainty, and would greatly appreciate your support. If you come across any openings or simply wish to connect, feel free to send me an email.
