IT University of Copenhagen

Mohsen Ghaffari is a postdoctoral researcher in Computer Science at SQUARE group, IT-University of Copenhagen, Denmark. He received a PhD degree in Computer Science from ITU. His research focuses on using formal methods to improve the reliability, safety, and explainability of Reinforcement Learning. He also explores decision-making under uncertainty and the applications of Multi-Agent Reinforcement Learning in real-world problems, particularly in smart grids and route planning.
